## When you need brake repair

It usually starts as a sound. A high-pitched squeal as you pull up at the lights, or a grinding when you brake harder than usual. Sometimes the pedal feels a little softer than it used to. Sometimes the car pulls to one side under braking, or a dash light comes on. Any of these mean it is time to get the brakes checked.

Brakes are the one part of the car where leaving it gets exponentially more expensive. New pads on worn rotors saves nothing, because the rotors chew through the new pads in months. Worn pads damage rotors. Worn rotors damage calipers. By the time the pedal is travelling near the floor, you are looking at a complete corner overhaul that costs three times what a routine pad replacement would have.

## What's included

A standard front brake job from us includes:

- Removal of the wheels and a full inspection of the brake hardware
- New brake pads, sized and graded for your vehicle. We carry OEM or quality aftermarket pads, sized and graded for your vehicle.
- New rotors when they are out of spec. We measure with a micrometer rather than guessing.
- Caliper inspection, slide-pin clean and re-grease
- Brake fluid top-up
- A road test in your area to bed the new pads in and verify pedal feel

If we find anything else worth flagging during the job (a leaking caliper, a worn flexible hose, a stuck slide pin), we stop and call you with a fixed quote before doing extra work. Nothing gets added to the bill without your agreement.
