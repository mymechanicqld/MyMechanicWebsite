## When you need a new battery

A car battery does not usually warn you nicely. It goes flat the morning of an interstate flight, before the school run, or in the work car park on the way home. Catching it early saves the stress.

The clearest signs your battery is on the way out:

- The engine cranks slow on cold mornings
- Headlights dim noticeably when the engine is idling
- The battery warning light flickers on, especially under load
- A start-stop car keeps refusing to stop the engine in traffic (the system protects the battery)
- You have needed a jump-start in the last six months

If any of these are happening and the battery is over three years old, replace it before it strands you.

## What's included

A standard mobile battery replacement from us includes:

- A diagnostic check before we change anything (state-of-charge, state-of-health, alternator output) so we confirm the battery is the actual fault
- A new heavy-duty battery from a quality replacement battery, sized for your vehicle and matched to its electrical load
- Battery terminal clean and re-grease
- Reset of the vehicle's idle and electrical settings where needed (modern cars often need this)
- Disposal of the old battery for proper recycling

If we find the battery is fine and the actual fault is the alternator or a parasitic drain, we will tell you and quote that work separately. We do not replace a healthy battery to make a sale.
