## When you need a pre-purchase inspection

Almost any time you are paying real money for a used car. A pre-purchase inspection costs less than a single mistake on a used buy, and it is the cheapest insurance you will ever take out.

The situations where it matters most:

- Buying privately on Facebook Marketplace, Carsales or Gumtree (no statutory warranty, and the seller can disappear)
- Buying a vehicle that has been sitting unused for a while
- Buying any used car of meaningful value, where one missed fault can outweigh many inspections
- Buying a 4WD or ute that has been used hard (towing, off-road, on a building site)
- Buying any vehicle you cannot easily drive to a workshop yourself

The exception is genuine dealer warranty cars from major brands, where the warranty itself is the protection. Even then, an inspection on a high-value SUV is cheap peace of mind.

## What's included

Our comprehensive pre-purchase inspection covers:

**Under the bonnet**

- Engine condition, leaks, mounts, drive belt and tensioner
- Cooling system inspection and pressure check
- Battery state-of-health and alternator output
- Full OBD scan for stored and pending fault codes

**Underneath**

- Transmission, drivetrain, CV joints, differential
- Suspension components, ball joints, bushes, shocks
- Steering rack, tie-rod ends, power-steering condition
- Brakes (pad and rotor measurement at all four corners)
- Exhaust system, catalytic converter, signs of repair welding
- Underbody rust check, especially on the chassis rails

**Body and interior**

- Body panel alignment (check for previous accident repair)
- Paint condition and overspray check
- All electrics: lights, indicators, dash, windows, air-con cold-test
- Interior condition, seat-belt operation, airbag indicator history
- Tyres, including spare, and wheel condition

**On the road**

- Cold start and warm-up behaviour
- Drive cycle including a freeway run where possible
- Brake feel under hard stop
- Transmission shift quality under load
- Any unusual noises or vibrations

You get a written report with photos, the codes we found, the road-test results, and a plain-English summary of what we would buy, what we would negotiate, and what we would walk away from.
