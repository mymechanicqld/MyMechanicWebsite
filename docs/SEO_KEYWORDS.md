# SEO Keyword Research

The full keyword strategy. Companion to [SEO_PLAYBOOK.md](SEO_PLAYBOOK.md) and [CONTENT_STRATEGY.md](CONTENT_STRATEGY.md).

The principle: **people search what is wrong, not what they want to buy**. Someone with a flat battery types "car won't start in the morning", not "mobile battery replacement Brisbane". Our SEO has to capture both ends.

Keywords are grouped by intent type. Within each group, items are roughly in priority order. The "target page" column points to where on the site we currently rank (or should rank) for that phrase.

## Anchor text variety

For internal links pointing at the four priority service pages, use a variety of anchor texts (Google distrusts repeated anchor text):

**/brake-repairs/ anchors to vary across pages:**

- brake repair
- mobile brake repair
- brake repair service
- brake pad and rotor replacement
- brake job in Brisbane
- our brake repair service
- the brake repair service page

**/starter-alternator/ anchors:**

- alternator and starter motor service
- charging-system repair
- mobile alternator replacement
- alternator repair Brisbane
- won't-start diagnostic
- starter motor replacement

**/radiator-cooling-system/ anchors:**

- cooling-system repair
- radiator and water pump service
- overheating diagnostic
- water pump replacement
- mobile cooling-system repair

**/logbook-servicing/ anchors:**

- logbook servicing
- mobile logbook service
- scheduled service
- manufacturer-spec service
- car servicing
- general service

Don't use the same anchor text 10 times for the same destination. Google reads that as link-building manipulation.

## Ongoing keyword work

Monthly:

- Pull the top 50 queries from Google Search Console
- Cross-reference against this list. Surprises go into the doc.
- For any query the site ranks 11–20 for, flag the corresponding page for a content refresh

Quarterly:

- Re-check competitor (Auto King) for any new pages ranking that we don't cover
- Add or refine target keywords based on actual data

The point of this document is to make keyword strategy explicit, so anyone writing new content knows what they are targeting and how to write naturally toward it.
