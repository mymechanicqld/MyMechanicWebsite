A note from the workshop

Why we built this around the customer.

I spent fifteen years inside dealership workshops, watching the same thing happen most days.

A customer drops the car at 8am. Takes an Uber to work. Refreshes their phone all day. Comes back at 5 to a bill that's somehow $400 over the quote, and a service advisor who can't quite explain why.

That isn't how I'd want my own family treated. So I bought a van, kitted it out with the same diagnostic gear and the same parts I had in the workshop, and started turning up at people's houses instead.

Every job still goes through me or one of my mechanics. The price we quote is the price you pay. We use proper parts, Bosch, Bendix, Ryco, not whatever the wholesaler had on special this week. And we back every repair with a 12-month, 20,000 km workmanship warranty.

If something on your car isn't right, you can call me on the number below. That part isn't a marketing line.
