Why customers choose us

Three things you can count on, every job.

Most car-repair frustration comes from the same three things: surprise pricing, vague explanations, and "while we were in there" upselling. We don't do those.

Fixed price, written up front

You see the full price before we lift a spanner. If something else turns up during the job, we stop and call you first.

12-month workmanship warranty

Every repair is backed by a 12-month or 20,000 km warranty. If something fails on our work, we come back and fix it for free.

Warranty-safe logbook servicing

Qualified mechanic, manufacturer-spec parts and fluids, dated logbook stamp. Your new-car warranty is protected under Australian Consumer Law.

Why I started doing this from a van.

I built this business so my own family wouldn't get treated that way. We come to the customer. We quote the job before we start it. We use the same diagnostic gear and OEM parts a dealership does. And every repair is signed off with a 12-month warranty.
