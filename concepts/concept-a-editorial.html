A note from the owner

Why we drive to you.

I spent fifteen years inside dealership workshops. Long enough to watch the same thing happen most days. A customer would drop the car at 8am, take an Uber to work, refresh their phone all day, and come back at 5 to a bill that was somehow $400 more than the original quote.

That isn't how I'd want my own family treated. So I bought a van, kitted it out with the same diagnostic gear and parts I had in the workshop, and started turning up at people's houses instead.

Every job still goes through me or one of my mechanics. The quote you see before we start is the quote you pay. We use Bosch, Bendix and Ryco, not whatever the wholesaler had on special. And we back every repair with our 12-month, 20,000 km warranty.

If something on your car isn't right, you can call me on the number below. That part isn't a marketing line.
